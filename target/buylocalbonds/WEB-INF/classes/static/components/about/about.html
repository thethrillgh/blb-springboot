About Buy Local Bonds


Buy Local Bonds™ is a modern marketplace for people to learn about and invest in bonds. United States Treaury bonds are one of the most traded asets in the country and now consumers can learn and participate in trading bonds. This is a consumer friendly application tailored to individual consumers. For that reason we encourage trades of as small as 1 bond to as many as you are willing to purchase.

New to bonds and/or trading? Head over to our Help page for resources and tips for maximizing your investments.
